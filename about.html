About Bahost Dev Academy

Bahost Dev Academy is an independent educational website created by Bruno "BaHost". The mission is to teach Roblox development with long-form, practical, and policy-safe learning content focused on scripting quality and real software engineering practices.

Editorial focus

  • Lua fundamentals and structured learning for beginners.
  • UI architecture and reusable component design.
  • XMCLib tutorials and maintainable coding workflows.
  • Ethical development guidance with no exploit or bypass content.

Author identity

Bruno "BaHost" is a Roblox UI-focused developer and maintainer of educational scripts. This website exists to share technical knowledge and support creators who want to build stable, player-friendly experiences.
